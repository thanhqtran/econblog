Quang-Thanh Tran

(pronounced "quant-thank chUN" (as in "SUN")).

is a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at Tohoku University. You can call him Tedd.

His primary research interests include macroeconomics, economic growth, overlapping generations model, and computational economics. He has been working on economic aging and now want to expand his research to other topics such as: automation and skill premium, developing traps, middle-income traps, and climate change.

Quang-Thanh earned his BA in International Economics at the Foreign Trade University (Vietnam) and his Master's at Tohoku University (Japan).

He is currently the representative of the Economics Graduate Student Association at Tohoku University (inseikai) and the Vietnamese Student Association at Tohoku University (VSA Tohoku).

He likes blogging, painting , and reading (especially science-fiction and history).
